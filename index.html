Little Learners Playground

Tap, talk, and wiggle to learn!

Build v17 · true-monkey
👋 Hi parents! This game practices skills common in preschool & kindergarten admission interviews: letter names and phonic sounds, tracing, early spelling, one-to-one counting, colors, shapes, speaking clearly, and answering questions about themselves in full sentences.

⭐ Every 5 stars unlocks a sticker — progress is saved on this device.
🎤 Speaking games use the microphone; 📷 Move & Pop uses the camera. Nothing is recorded or uploaded — it all runs locally.
💡 Works best in Chrome with sound on. Animal sounds are real recordings from Wikimedia Commons (CC/public-domain).

🔒 Camera & microphone games (Say It, Interview, Move & Pop, Big Voice) only work when this game is opened from a website address (https://…) — browsers block camera/mic for files opened directly. Free hosting like GitHub Pages or Netlify fixes this in minutes; the tap/trace games work fine either way.

🗣️ Voice:
Tip: voices with “Natural” or “Google” in the name sound the friendliest.
